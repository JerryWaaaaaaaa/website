### Integration notes for Curry
- **Large video asset**: `meeting-floating.mp4` is ~16 MB. In production, serve it from a CDN or transcode to WebM/AV1 with aggressive compression; consider lazy-loading or `IntersectionObserver` to defer download until the hero is near-viewport.
- **Rotation sync**: The JS `ROTATION_INTERVAL` (2200ms) must match the CSS `--rotation-interval` custom property driving `hero-rotating` keyframes in the shared CSS — if you change one, change both.
- **Icon paths are hardcoded**: Product icons reference `/Icon/product-slides.svg`, `/Icon/product-sheet.svg`, etc. Verify these exist in your production static-asset pipeline or replace with bundled imports.
- **Prototype-only patterns**: The ternary hero switch in `Marketing.tsx` and the `?hero=v3` query-param toggle are for A/B review — production should pick one variant and remove the toggle infrastructure (`HeroVariantToggle.tsx`, the `useSearchParams` branching).
