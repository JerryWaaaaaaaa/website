### Integration notes for Curry
- **Prototype-only tooling to drop**: `SliderTuner`, `BezierEditor`, the `sliderDefaultsWriter` Vite plugin, the `/__save-slider-defaults` endpoint, and the `HeroVariantToggle` (V1–V4 switcher) are all prototyping/dev UX — do not ship. Once you've locked in the `SLIDER_DEFAULTS` values, hardcode them directly in `BuiltDifferentiate`.
- **Placeholder assets**: Hub, Mail, and Calendar tabs in HeroV4 all point to the same `canvas-ui.png` stand-in (`PLACEHOLDER_SCREEN`). Real screenshots are needed before launch. UseCase images are delivered in both `.webp` and `.png` — the component currently uses WebP only; consider adding `<picture>` with PNG fallback.
- **HeroV4 ribbon anchoring**: The `useLayoutEffect` + `ResizeObserver` in HeroV4 dynamically recomputes a flat SVG cubic-bezier path to anchor the transcript ribbon to the product screen. This is fragile and couples to the `HeroTranscript` SVG's hardcoded `1440 × 290` viewBox. If the ribbon design changes, update both the viewBox constants and the `toVbX`/`toVbY` math.
- **BuiltDifferentiate media**: Active cards render `MediaPlaceholder` (a gradient-fill stand-in from the design system). Replace with real product screenshots or video embeds per the final creative brief.
