### Integration notes for Curry
- **Install `dialkit ^1.3.0`** — it powers the entire variant/tuning UI. The `DialRoot` panel, `V5Tuning` sliders, and `useDialKit` selects are **prototype-only authoring tooling**; in production strip them and hard-code the chosen variant (currently Hero=`b`/Grouped, plus whatever URL params the design link carries). `sharp` is a devDep used for image processing, not runtime.
- **`public/product-videos/` is git-ignored and the Slides/Canvas MP4s were deleted** — the large slides MP4 is gone because Slides is now the DOM mockup, but `ProductSuiteV5` still references `Sheets-%20finalized%20version.mp4` and other product videos that are **not in the repo**. Curry will need those video assets from Jerry to render the non-Slides tabs.
- **`keyPoints.tsx` is locked** (can't be read/edited) — that's why the meeting-deliverable animation is swapped in by matching `media.src` inside `StepMedia.tsx` rather than by adding a clean `layers`/`animation` field. In production this should be a first-class media kind in the data model, not a src-string interception.
- **SlidesMockup carries hardcoded prototype data** — the 5-avatar collaborator stack points at `/avatars/*.png`, slide count is fixed at 8, and the animation timing/positions are tuned constants. The fixed 1280×720 stage scaling and window-level pointer listener are deliberate (see in-file comments); preserve the `prefers-reduced-motion` branch when rebuilding.
